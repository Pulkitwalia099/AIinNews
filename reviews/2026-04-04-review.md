### Recommendation 1: Deduplicate articles across consecutive issues
**Type:** bug_fix | **Priority:** high | **Effort:** small

**Why:** Two separate readers independently reported the same issue — articles reappearing day over day — which is the strongest signal available in the feedback set. Story repetition directly erodes perceived value and is a likely driver of the 51% thumbs-down rate.

**What to build:** Add a deduplication layer to the article selection pipeline that stores a rolling 7-day cache of published article URLs (or canonical content hashes). **Expected impact:** Expected to reduce repeated-content complaints to zero and improve approval rate by 5-10pp within two weeks by ensuring every issue feels genuinely fresh.
